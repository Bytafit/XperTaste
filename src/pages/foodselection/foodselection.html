Instructions

Pour 1 1/2 cups warm water into a large bowl; sprinkle with yeast and let stand until foamy, about 5 minutes. Whisk sugar, oil, and salt into yeast mixture. Add flour and stir until a sticky dough forms. ... To freeze, you can wrap the dough in plastic and freeze it in a resealable freezer bag for up to 3 months.
